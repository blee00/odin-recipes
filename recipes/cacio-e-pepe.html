Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e pepe has its roots in Lazio, Italy. The name cacio e pepe literally means "cheese and pepper" which gives insight to the dish's simple ingredients. Both ingredients actually have long shelf lives providing shephards, without permanent residency, a practical meal.

Interestingly, this dish's minimalism is its greatest potential; cacio e pepe serves as the quintessential pasta dish, add cured pork jowl, or guanciale, and the dish becomes pasta alla gricia; you can then add an emulsified egg sauce and you have carbonara. Alternatively, instead of eggs, you add tomatoes and you'll get amatriciana. This dish is amazing because of its basic ingredients.
